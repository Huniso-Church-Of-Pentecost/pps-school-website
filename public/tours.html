Our History

Built Over Decades

The story of how our campus grew into what it is today.

Founded

🏫 School Established

Pentecost Preparatory School opened its doors with a vision to provide faith-based, quality education to the Western Region community of Ghana.

Expansion

💻 ICT Centre Inaugurated

A state-of-the-art computer lab was built, bringing digital education to students years ahead of many regional schools.

Development

⚽ Sports Field & Hall Built

The construction of our sports field and school hall gave students space to thrive beyond academics — in sports, arts, and culture.

Today

🏆 A Complete Modern Campus

PPS now boasts 9 major facilities serving 1,200+ students — a testament to decades of community investment and unwavering excellence.
